Fullscreen fixed header

This is a demonstrates the "fullscreen" toolbar mode. It works just like the default "fixed" toolbar mode except that the toolbars aren't shown at the top and bottom of the page and only appear when the page is clicked. This is useful for immersive apps like photo or video viewers where you want the content to full when whole screen and toolbars can be summoned to appear by tapping the screen. Keep in mind that the toolbars in this mode will sit over page content so not all content will be accessible with the toolbars open, just like in this situation.
